    4. Both A and B

Short Answer
  1. Describe the key steps to safely use shared left-turn lanes. Key steps to safely use a shared left-turn lane is to only use the lane to turn left, don't get in the lane too soon, watch for vehicles pulling out of driveways and cross streets, yeild right of way to any vehicle that is signaling to enter or that's already the lane.
  2. Explain the meaning of a signle light's three colors. Red means to stop prior to the crosswalk, stop bar, or roadway-edge line. Make sure you DO NOT enter the roadway. Yellow means the right of way is expiring and the driver needs to clear the intersection (Red light will follow immediately). Green means when the traffic has cleared, and it is safe to maneuver, you may enter the intersection.
  3. What information is communicated by each of the following sign colors?
    1. Green - Directional guidance; indicates movement is permitted
    2. Yellow - General warning/caution
    3. Red - Stop or prohibition
  4. What information is communicated by each of the following sign colors?
    1. Blue - Motorist services guidance
    2. Brown - Recreational and points-of-interest guidance
    3. Orange - Temporary work zone
  5. Describe the differences in meaning between white and yellow road markings. White lines often define lanes of traffic going in the same direction, or they show the driver the location of the shoulder of the road. Yellow lines often show the driver that traffic is going in different directions.
