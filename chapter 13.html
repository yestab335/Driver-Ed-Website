    2. Reducing your level of alertness
    3. Causing drowsiness

Short Answer
  1. Explain how marijuana can affect a person's driving ability. Marijuana can affect a person's driving ability because the driver tends to focus on one thing at a time.
  2. List the effects that depressant drugs have on the body. Slows down the central nervous system, slowed perception of a traffic scene, delayed decision-making skills, and a reduced ability to control steering and vehicle speed.
  3. Explain how a temporary illness can affect driving safety. For a cold, it could cause a driver to miss seeing something important or notice it too late. Slow down a driver's decision-making abou tsomething they saw within a traffic scene. Slow down reaction time when needing to change speed or position in response to a traffic issue.
  4. List the effects that stimulant drugs have on the body. It speeds up the centeral nervous system and can cause serious drowsiness.
  5. List three ways in which you can protect yourself and others from the dangers of drugs. Talk to a close individual, join a support group, and simply don't mix drugs and driving.
