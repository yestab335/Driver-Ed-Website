  2. Explain how different types of beverages can contain different amounts of pure alcohol. Beer contains 5% while others can vary.
  3. List and explain the three ways alcohol is eliminated by the body. The three ways alcohol is eliminiated by the body is by breath, prespiring (sweat), and oxidation.
  4. Describe the effects of alcohol on vision. When effects of alcohol on vision is that it gets blurry when someone is drunk. The driver is unable to see that many colors because of how blurry it is.
  5. Explain how different levels of alcohol contribute to impaired body function. Different levels of alcohol contribute to impaired body function because it slows the driver's actions.
